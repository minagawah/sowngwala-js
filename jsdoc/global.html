days_since_1990(year) → {DecimalDays}

Find out days since 1990. However, whatever the date is given, it will compare two dates by 'Jan 0th'. For instance, when 'July 27th, 1988' is given, then it will compare 'Jan0th, 1988' and 'Jan0th, 1990' which is exactly -2 years. Since we have a leap year on 1988, as to for days, we subtract -1. (Peter Duffett-Smith, p.86) Given: July 27th, 1988 365 days * 2 yrs = 730 days 730 days - 1 day = 729 days Since it 1988 is before 1990: 720 days * -1 = -729 days Original: - sowngwala::time::days_since_1990
